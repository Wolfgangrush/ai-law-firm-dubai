## Dual-system positioning

AI Law Firm — Dubai covers Dubai's **dual legal system**:

- **DIFC** (Dubai International Financial Centre) — common-law jurisdiction with its own courts, laws, and regulator (DFSA + DIFC-DPC)
- **Onshore Dubai / Federal UAE** — civil-law jurisdiction governed by UAE Federal Law and Dubai local laws

The tool flags every output with the applicable legal system and warns against cross-system carryover (DIFC concepts do not transplant cleanly to onshore matters and vice versa).

The UAE PDPL imposes obligations on Data Controllers and Data Processors processing personal data of UAE data subjects.

The publisher is **neither a Controller nor a Processor** under the PDPL with respect to your tool usage — the publisher operates no server processing user input. See [NO_PII_NO_DATA.md](NO_PII_NO_DATA.md) for the complete zero-collection architecture.
