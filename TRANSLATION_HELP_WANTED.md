## How to help

1. Read your language guide
2. Fix any errors in legal terminology or natural phrasing
3. Submit a PR to `https://github.com/Wolfgangrush/ai-law-firm-dubai`

## Why these 5 languages?

Dubai's legal community is multilingual:

- **English** — DIFC working language; expat lawyers from UK, Australia, Singapore
- **العربية** — Mainland court proceedings; UAE national advocates
- **اردو** — Large Pakistani expat legal community
- **हिन्दी** — Large Indian expat community (lawyers, paralegals, clients)
- **Tagalog** — Large Filipino workforce in legal-support roles (paralegals, admin, court staff)

## Quality disclaimer

Arabic is the only non-English guide marked "native quality" — it was anchored to the 35 Arabic-language research files about UAE/Dubai law. Urdu, Hindi, and Tagalog are AI-assisted; we welcome corrections from native speakers.

## Format

Each guide follows the same structure as GETTING_STARTED.md:
1. Welcome greeting in language
2. What is this?
3. Installation
4. Quick start
5. Supported courts
6. Compliance features
7. License

Keep translations under 200 lines. Focus on clarity over completeness.
