## Language Variants
English, Arabic/English Dual

## Currency Warning
None.

## Template Type
Board Resolution

## Structural Skeleton
1. Company Name and Registration No.
2. Date and Venue of Meeting
3. Present Directors
4. Agenda Items
5. Resolutions (The Decisions)
6. Signature of Directors/Chairman

## Drafting Conventions
- Must comply with the Quorum requirements in the MOA/AOA.
- Use "RESOLVED THAT..." format.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"RESOLVED THAT the Company enters into the Agreement with [Party] and that [Name] is hereby authorized to sign the Agreement on behalf of the Company."

## Practical Use
Authorizing corporate actions for entities in DIFC or Mainland.
