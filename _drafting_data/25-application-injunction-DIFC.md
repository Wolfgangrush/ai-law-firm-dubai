## Drafting Conventions
- Address the "American Cyanamid" principles: Serious question to be tried; Balance of convenience; Adequacy of damages.
- Duty of full and frank disclosure is paramount.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Defendant shall not, until further order of the Court, [Prohibited Act]. The Claimant provides an undertaking to the Court to pay any damages the Defendant suffers if it is later found this Order should not have been made."

## Practical Use
