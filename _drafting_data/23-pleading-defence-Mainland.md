## Template Type
Pleading - Defence (Mudhakkirat al-Difa')

## Structural Skeleton
1. Header (Case No., Date of Hearing)
2. Preliminary Objections (Jurisdiction, Capacity)
3. Response to Facts
4. Legal Rebuttal (Distinguishing Articles)
5. Requests for Dismissal
6. Signature/Power of Attorney Reference

## Drafting Conventions
- Categorical denial of incorrect facts.
- Emphasis on "Al-Difa' al-Mawdu'i" (Merit-based defence) vs "Al-Difa' al-Shakli" (Formal/Procedural defence).

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Defendant requests the court to: Primarily: Dismiss the case for lack of jurisdiction. Secondarily: Dismiss the case on its merits and charge the Claimant with all costs and attorney fees."

## Practical Use
Defending actions in Dubai Mainland Courts.
