## System: DIFC

## Authority Tier
DIFC Courts Law No. 10 of 2004 + DIFC Small Claims Tribunal Rules + RDC Part 53

## Language Variants
English (Primary)

## Currency Warning
DIFC Small Claims Tribunal (SCT) jurisdiction for claims up to AED 500,000 (verify current — periodically updated). Streamlined procedure with mandatory pre-filing conciliation. Cost-effective for low-to-medium-value commercial disputes connected to DIFC.

## Template Type
Tribunal Form + Statement

## Structural Skeleton

**Claim Form**

1. **Online filing** via DIFC Courts e-Registry.
2. **Applicant identification** — name · Emirates ID / passport · DIFC company details (if corporate) · contact details.
3. **Respondent identification** — DIFC company / DIFC-registered branch.
4. **Claim particulars**:
   - Sum claimed in AED (or USD with AED conversion).
   - Cause of action — contract · debt · tort · employment.
   - Brief factual narrative.
5. **DIFC connection** — applicant + respondent's DIFC nexus (jurisdictional gateway).
6. **Reliefs sought** — judgment for sum · interest · costs.
7. **Filing fee** — DIFC SCT scale (typically 5% of claim value with floor + cap).
8. **Annexures** — contracts · invoices · correspondence.

**SCT Procedure**

1. **Pre-Trial Conciliation** — mandatory (RDC 53.30); SCT Registrar attempts settlement.
2. **First Hearing** — usually within 4-6 weeks; informal · oral · adjudicator-led.
3. **Documentary evidence** — submitted at first hearing.
4. **Judgment** — typically delivered within days of hearing.
5. **Appeal** — to DIFC Court of First Instance on questions of law (with leave); to DIFC Court of Appeal in limited circumstances.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Jurisdiction gateway** — DIFC SCT jurisdiction requires DIFC connection (parties, contract, dispute location).
- **AED 500,000 cap** — claims above must go to DIFC Court of First Instance.
- **Self-represented friendly** — designed for lay applicants; legal representation permitted.
- **Pre-trial conciliation mandatory** — non-attendance triggers adverse costs.
- **English** — proceedings in English; translation of foreign-language documents.
- **No formal pleadings** — claim form is the primary document.
- **Enforcement** — SCT judgment enforceable as DIFC judgment + onshore via DIFC-Dubai Protocol.
- **Common SCT cases** — employment (small DIFC employer claims) · consumer disputes (DIFC retail / services) · landlord-tenant (DIFC residential property) · contract disputes under AED 500k.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Small Claims Tribunal has jurisdiction to hear and determine claims connected to the DIFC where the value does not exceed the prescribed limit. The Tribunal applies a simplified procedure and seeks to dispose of claims expeditiously." (DIFC Courts Law + RDC Part 53, paraphrased.)

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
DIFC SCT is the principal forum for low-to-medium-value commercial disputes connected to DIFC. Solo practitioners can appear or advise pre-filing; mandatory conciliation often resolves matters. Streamlined procedure + rapid resolution + cost-effective filing fees make SCT attractive alternative to mainstream DIFC Court of First Instance for sub-AED 500k matters.
