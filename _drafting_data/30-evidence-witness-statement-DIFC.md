## Template Type
Evidence - Witness Statement

## Structural Skeleton
1. Header (Parties, No., Witness Name, No. of Statement)
2. Witness Details (Full name, address, occupation)
3. Source of Information (Personal knowledge or hearsay)
4. Narrative (Chronological account of facts)
5. Exhibits (List of documents referred to)
6. Statement of Truth (RDC 29.33)

## Drafting Conventions
- Must be in the witness's own words.
- Use the first person ("I").
- Numbered paragraphs.
- Focus on facts, not legal argument.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"I, [Name], of [Address], will say as follows: 1. I am the [Position] of the Claimant. 2. I make this statement in support of the Claimant’s claim. 3. The facts in this statement are within my own knowledge except where I state otherwise."

## Practical Use
Providing factual evidence for trials or applications in DIFC Courts.
