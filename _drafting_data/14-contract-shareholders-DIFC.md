- Alignment with DIFC Companies Law on director duties.
- Clarity on "Fair Value" mechanisms for share transfers.
- Conflict of interest provisions.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"No Shareholder shall transfer any of its Shares to a third party without first offering such Shares to the other Shareholders in proportion to their existing shareholdings."

## Practical Use
Joint ventures and private equity investments in DIFC entities.
