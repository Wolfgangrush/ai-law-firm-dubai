## Template Type
Contractual Clause - Arbitration

## Structural Skeleton
1. Agreement to Arbitrate
2. Scope of Disputes (All/Some)
3. Institutional Rules (DIAC Rules 2022)
4. Number of Arbitrators (One or Three)
5. Seat of Arbitration (DIFC or Mainland Dubai)
6. Language of Arbitration
7. Governing Law of the Contract

## Drafting Conventions
- Use the Model Clause provided by the institution (DIAC).
- Clearly distinguish between "Seat" (Legal Jurisdiction) and "Venue" (Physical location).

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this contract, including any question regarding its existence, validity or termination, shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration under the Arbitration Rules of the Dubai International Arbitration Centre."

## Practical Use
Ensuring dispute resolution outside the court system.
