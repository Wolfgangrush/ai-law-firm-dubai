## System
## System: DIFC

## Authority Tier
Court Rules (RDC Part 16)

## Language Variants
English (Primary)

## Currency Warning
Strict timelines under RDC 16.2.

## Template Type
Pleading - Defence

## Structural Skeleton
1. Header (Match Claim)
2. Admissions (RDC 17.41)
3. Denials (Explicitly state reasons)
4. Non-admissions
5. Positive Case / Alternative Version
6. Set-off / Counterclaim (if any)
7. Statement of Truth

## Drafting Conventions
- Must respond to every allegation in the Statement of Claim.
- Failure to deny is often treated as admission.
- Include specific RDC references where applicable.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"Save as expressly admitted or non-admitted below, the Defendant denies each and every allegation in the Statement of Claim as if the same were set out herein and specifically traversed."

## Practical Use
Responding to a DIFC Court claim within the 14/28 day deadline.
