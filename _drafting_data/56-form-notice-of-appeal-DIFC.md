## Currency Warning
Strict time limits for Appellant's Notice (RDC 38.16).

## Template Type
Court Form - Appellant's Notice

## Structural Skeleton
1. Header (Court of Appeal)
2. Details of the Order being appealed
3. Grounds of Appeal (Errors of Law/Fact)
4. Skeleton Argument (Summary)
5. Request for Permission to Appeal (if required)

## Drafting Conventions
- Grounds must be specific.
- Reference the specific paragraphs of the judgment being challenged.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Appellant appeals against the Order of [Judge] dated [Date] on the grounds that the learned Judge erred in law by [Reason]."

## Practical Use
Challenging a first-instance decision in the DIFC Court of Appeal.
