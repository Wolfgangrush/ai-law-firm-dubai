## System
## System: Mainland

## Authority Tier
Primary Statute (Federal Decree-Law No. 42 of 2022 + Dubai Courts Procedural Rules)

## Language Variants
Arabic (Mandatory)

## Currency Warning
Mainland Dubai Courts hearings proceed through written memoranda filed before each hearing — there is NO equivalent to common-law trial bundle or written closing submissions. Each hearing memorandum (مذكرة الجلسة) addresses the issues raised since last hearing.

## Template Type
Hearings Memorandum (مذكرة)

## Structural Skeleton

**Hearing Memorandum** (مذكرة الجلسة)

1. **Heading** — Court of First Instance · Dubai Courts · case number · year · parties.
2. **Title** — "Memorandum of [Claimant / Defendant] for Hearing of [Date]".
3. **Procedural history** — brief summary since last memorandum (or from inception for first memo).
4. **Issues raised at last hearing** — court's questions · opposing memoranda · court orders.
5. **Substantive submissions**:
   - **Claims pleaded** — concise restatement.
   - **Statutory framework** — UAE Civil Code provisions · Federal Decree-Law 50/2022 · sector-specific.
   - **Application of law to facts**.
   - **Evidence relied upon** — documents · sworn statements · court-appointed Expert report.
   - **Response to opposing memorandum** (if applicable).
6. **Reliefs sought** at current hearing — interim orders · production · expert appointment · judgment.
7. **Annexures** — supporting documents (Arabic + legalised translations of foreign documents).
8. **Signature** — UAE-licensed advocate.

**Final Memorandum** (المذكرة الختامية)

1. **Title** — "Final Memorandum of [Party]".
2. **Comprehensive summary** of party's case:
   - Cause of action / defence.
   - Statutory framework + interpretation.
   - All evidence on file (pleadings · sworn statements · expert reports · documents).
3. **Application to facts**.
4. **Response to all opposing party submissions**.
5. **Final relief sought**:
   - Judgment for `<sum>` in AED + interest + costs + attorney's fees.
6. **Annexures** — comprehensive bundle.
7. **Signature**.

**Expert Report Comments Memorandum** (تعقيب على تقرير الخبير)

- Filed after court-appointed expert report.
- Either acceptance or challenge.
- Procedural / qualification / substantive grounds for challenge.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Arabic mandatory** — all hearing memoranda in Arabic.
- **Sequential structure** — each memorandum addresses developments since last.
- **No oral closings** — written final memorandum substitutes.
- **Court-appointed Expert** dominant — most commercial disputes turn on Expert report (file 74).
- **Pleadings + sworn statements + Expert** = the trial record; no transcripts of oral examination.
- **Federal Decree-Law 50/2022 (Commercial Transactions)** — interest provisions · commercial-contract interpretation.
- **UAE Civil Code (Federal Law 5/1985)** — general contract law · tort · unjust enrichment.
- **Federal Decree-Law 32/2021 (Commercial Companies)** — corporate matters.
- **Federal Decree-Law 33/2021 (Labour)** — employment matters.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The court shall hear the parties and review the written memoranda submitted at each hearing. The court may direct the parties to submit a final memorandum addressing all issues in dispute before judgment is rendered." (Federal Decree-Law No. 42 of 2022, paraphrased.)

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Mainland Dubai Courts practice is memorandum-driven. Solo practitioners working with UAE-licensed advocates must brief them carefully on factual + legal positions for each memorandum. The Final Memorandum substitutes for common-law written closing; comprehensive treatment of all issues + responses to opposing memoranda required. Court-appointed Expert report is typically the decisive evidence — Expert Comments Memorandum is critical strategic document.
