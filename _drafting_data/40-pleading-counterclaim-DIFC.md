## Template Type
Pleading - Counterclaim

## Structural Skeleton
1. Header (Match Claim)
2. Title: "Defence and Counterclaim"
3. Counterclaim Details (Causes of Action)
4. Linkage to the Main Claim
5. Loss and Damage
6. Prayer for Relief (The Counterclaim)
7. Statement of Truth

## Drafting Conventions
- Must be filed with the Defence unless the Court gives permission (RDC 20.7).
- Treated as a separate claim for the purposes of court fees.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Defendant counterclaims against the Claimant for: 1. Damages for [Cause of Action]. 2. Interest. 3. Costs."

## Practical Use
Asserting a claim against the Claimant in the same proceedings.
