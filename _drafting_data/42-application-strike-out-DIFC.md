## System
## System: DIFC

## Authority Tier
Court Rules (RDC Part 4)

## Language Variants
English (Primary)

## Currency Warning
None.

## Template Type
Court Application - Strike Out

## Structural Skeleton
1. Application Notice
2. Grounds (RDC 4.16: no reasonable grounds, abuse of process, failure to comply with rules)
3. Witness Statement in Support
4. Draft Order

## Drafting Conventions
- High threshold: "Clearly bound to fail."
- Often combined with an application for Immediate Judgment.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Applicant applies for an order that the Statement of Claim be struck out on the grounds that it discloses no reasonable grounds for bringing the claim and/or is an abuse of the Court's process."

## Practical Use
Removing baseless or vexatious claims at an early stage.
