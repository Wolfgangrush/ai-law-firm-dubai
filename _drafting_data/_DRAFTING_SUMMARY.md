# DRAFTING DATA SUMMARY

## Project Overview
This dataset provides a comprehensive collection of drafting templates, court forms, and contract precedents for legal practitioners operating in the Dubai-DIFC dual-system jurisdiction.

## Key Coverage Areas
1. **Contracts:** Sale of Goods, Employment, Shareholders, NDA, Leasing, Professional Services, Distribution, Agency.
2. **Litigation (DIFC):** Statement of Claim, Defence, Counterclaim, Summary Judgment, Injunction, Strike Out, Witness Statements, Bill of Costs.
3. **Litigation (Mainland):** Statement of Claim, Defence, Counterclaim, Precautionary Attachment, Sworn Statements, Notifications.
4. **General & SHARED:** Letters Before Action, Arbitration Clauses, Settlement Agreements, Board Resolutions, Power of Attorney.

## Technical Standards
- **Systems:** Tagged by DIFC, Mainland, SHARED, or FOREIGN-LAW-PRACTICE.
- **Verification:** All entries verified as of 2026-05-19.
- **Currency:** Includes warnings for pre-2022 reforms in the Mainland system.

## Usage Note
These files are structural digests intended for integration into a Practice-OS software project. They focus on the architecture, primary source anchors, and drafting conventions rather than full-text reproduction, ensuring compliance with the COPYRIGHT FIREWALL mandate.
