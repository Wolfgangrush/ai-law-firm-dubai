# DRAFTING DATA SUMMARY — DUBAI-DIFC

## Project Overview
Comprehensive drafting templates, court forms, and contract precedents for legal practitioners operating in the Dubai-DIFC dual-system jurisdiction. Covers DIFC (English common law) + Mainland (Arabic civil law) + SHARED cross-system templates.

## Date: 2026-05-28
## Files produced: 81 templates (+ INDEX + SUMMARY = 83 .md files)
## Coverage: full litigation + commercial + tribunal backbone across DIFC + Mainland systems (v0.2 complete)

**Mainland**: Federal Decree-Law 42/2022 (Civil Procedure) · FDL 50/2022 (Commercial Transactions) · FDL 32/2021 (Commercial Companies) · FDL 33/2021 (Labour) + 9/2024 amendments · FDL 51/2023 (Bankruptcy + Insolvency) · Federal Law 6/2018 (Arbitration) · Federal Law 17/1978 (Administrative Disputes) · Federal Law 23/1991 (Legal Profession) · UAE Civil Code Federal Law 5/1985

**Institutional**: DIAC Rules 2022 · DIFC-LCIA legacy rules · DIFC Court Practice Directions · DFSA Rulebook · UNCITRAL Model Laws
