English (Primary)

## Currency Warning
Review against latest RDC amendments.

## Template Type
Pleading - Statement of Claim

## Structural Skeleton
1. Header (Court Name, Case No., Parties)
2. Introduction (Brief Summary)
3. The Parties
4. Jurisdiction (RDC 7.2)
5. Factual Background
6. Particulars of Breach / Cause of Action
7. Loss and Damage
8. Interest (Art. 38 DIFC Law of Damages)
9. Prayer for Relief (The Claim)
10. Statement of Truth (RDC Part 22)

## Drafting Conventions
- Must be concise and avoid evidence (RDC 17.1).
- Use numbered paragraphs.
- Specificity required for fraud or misrepresentation.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Claimant's claim is for damages for breach of contract. By an agreement in writing dated [Date], the Defendant agreed to [Terms]. In breach of the Agreement, the Defendant [Breach]. Consequently, the Claimant has suffered loss."

## Practical Use
Commencing civil proceedings in the DIFC Court of First Instance.
