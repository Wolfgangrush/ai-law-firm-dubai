## Currency Warning
Mainland UAE default-judgment regime under Federal Decree-Law No. 42/2022 (replacing the earlier Federal Law 11/1992). Default judgment (الحكم الغيابي) available where defendant duly served fails to appear at first hearing without lawful excuse. Defendant has right to seek opposition (المعارضة) — distinct remedy from common-law set-aside.

## Template Type
Procedural Application + Court Hearing Memorandum

## Structural Skeleton

**(A) HEARING MEMORANDUM (Memorandum of First Hearing)** — مذكرة الجلسة الأولى

1. **Heading** — Court of First Instance · Dubai Courts · case number · parties.
2. **Title** — "Memorandum of First Hearing — Application for Default Judgment".
3. **Service confirmation**:
   - Date of service of claim form on defendant.
   - Mode of service (personal · process server · publication).
   - Defendant's failure to appear without lawful excuse.
4. **Reliefs sought**:
   - Default judgment (حكم غيابي) on the claims pleaded.
   - Interest at legal rate (Federal Decree-Law 50/2022 + UAE Civil Code Art 88).
   - Costs + attorney's fees.
5. **Annexures** — Statement of Claim · service evidence · contractual documents.

**(B) DEFENDANT'S OPPOSITION (المعارضة)**

1. **Title** — "Opposition Against Default Judgment".
2. **Filing window** — typically 10 days from notice of default judgment (verify current Federal Decree-Law 42/2022 prescribed period).
3. **Grounds**:
   - Defective service (didn't receive papers).
   - Lawful excuse for non-appearance (illness · force majeure · administrative oversight).
   - Substantive defence to the claim.
4. **Procedure**:
   - Filed at the same court that issued default judgment.
   - Court re-opens proceedings if grounds established.
   - Substantive hearing on merits follows.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Arabic mandatory** — all court filings in Arabic.
- **Service requirements strict** — UAE service rules require either personal service or substituted service order from court.
- **Process server** — court-appointed process server (publication permitted only as last resort).
- **Out-of-jurisdiction service** — Hague Service Convention applies; UAE acceded.
- **Opposition vs Appeal** — opposition (المعارضة) re-opens at same court; appeal (الاستئناف) goes to Court of Appeal.
- **Costs of opposition** — generally borne by defendant on success of opposition; covered by court in final judgment.
- **Lawful excuse** — must be documented (medical certificate · travel record).
- **Substantive defence** — required for opposition to succeed; bare denial insufficient.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"If the defendant, having been duly served, fails to appear at the first hearing without lawful excuse, the court may issue a default judgment on the basis of the claimant's submissions. The defendant may file an opposition against the default judgment within the prescribed period." (Federal Decree-Law No. 42 of 2022, Articles 107-108, paraphrased.)

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Default judgment in mainland Dubai requires strict service compliance — defective service is the principal opposition ground. Solo practitioners must use court-appointed process server + obtain service confirmation document (محضر الإعلان). The opposition window is tight (typically 10 days from notice); defendants must move fast.
