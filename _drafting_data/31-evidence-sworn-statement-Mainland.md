## Currency Warning
2022 Evidence Law introduced significant electronic evidence provisions.

## Template Type
Evidence - Sworn Statement / Affidavit (Iqrar / Shahada)

## Structural Skeleton
1. Header (Court, Case No.)
2. Identification of the Deponent
3. The Oath (Al-Yamin)
4. Factual Assertions
5. Signature and Notarization (if out of court)

## Drafting Conventions
- Usually taken orally before the court or expert, but written statements must follow strict format.
- Electronic signatures are now explicitly recognized under Art. 53.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"I, the undersigned [Name], hereby swear by Almighty God that the following statements are true and correct to the best of my knowledge, and I am aware of the legal consequences of false testimony."

## Practical Use
Providing sworn testimony or declarations for use in Dubai Courts.
