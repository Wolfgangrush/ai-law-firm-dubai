## Template Type
Court Application - Joinder of Parties

## Structural Skeleton
1. Application Notice
2. Evidence in Support (Relationship between the parties and the claim)
3. Grounds for Joinder (Common question of law/fact)
4. Draft Order (Amending the Claim Form/Statement of Claim)

## Drafting Conventions
- Apply early to avoid trial delays.
- Ensure the new party has a real interest in the outcome.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Applicant applies for an order under RDC 20.27 that [Party Name] be joined as a Second Defendant to these proceedings on the basis that [Reason]."

## Practical Use
Bringing additional relevant parties into an existing DIFC lawsuit.
