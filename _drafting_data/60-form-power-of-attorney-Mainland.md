## Template Type
Power of Attorney (Wakala)

## Structural Skeleton
1. Principal's Details
2. Attorney's Details
3. Scope of Powers (Litigation, Management, Sales)
4. Duration (General or Special)
5. Right of Substitution
6. Notarial Authentication

## Drafting Conventions
- Must be notarized to be valid for court or government use.
- Use clear and specific language for "Special" powers.
- Standard "Litigation Wakala" has a set format recognized by the Ministry of Justice.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"I, the undersigned, hereby appoint [Name] as my attorney to represent me before all courts of all levels and types, and to sign all documents related to [Specific Matter]."

## Practical Use
Appointing legal counsel or business managers in mainland Dubai.
