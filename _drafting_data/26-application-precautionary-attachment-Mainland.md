## System: Mainland

## Authority Tier
Primary Statute (Federal Decree-Law No. 42 of 2022, Arts 111-120)

## Language Variants
Arabic (Mandatory)

## Currency Warning
2022 Law updated the procedures for "Orders on Petition" (Amr ala 'Aridha).

## Template Type
Court Application - Precautionary Attachment (Hajz Ihtiyati)

## Structural Skeleton
1. Petition to the Summary Judge
2. Proof of Debt (Must be certain and exigible)
3. Proof of Risk (Fear of asset dissipation)
4. Specification of Assets (Bank accounts, properties, cars)
5. Undertaking/Guarantee (often required by court)

## Drafting Conventions
- Must demonstrate "Qawa'id al-Istihqaq" (Rules of entitlement).
- Filed ex-parte.
- Highly formulaic and urgent.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Petitioner requests the honorable Summary Judge to issue an order for the precautionary attachment of the Respondent's assets at [Bank/Entity] up to the value of the claim amount [Amount]."

## Practical Use
Freezing assets in mainland Dubai prior to or during a lawsuit.
