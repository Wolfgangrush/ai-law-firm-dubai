## Template Type
Letter Before Action (LBA) / Formal Demand

## Structural Skeleton
1. Identification of Parties
2. Reference to the underlying Agreement/Incident
3. Precise details of the Breach/Debt
4. Clear Demand (Amount/Performance)
5. Deadline (Reasonable period, e.g., 7 or 14 days)
6. Warning of Legal Action
7. "Without Prejudice" (if applicable)

## Drafting Conventions
- Be firm but professional.
- Avoid inflammatory language.
- Ensure the deadline is realistic to avoid being seen as unreasonable by the court.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"This letter constitutes a formal demand for payment of the sum of [Amount] which is currently overdue. Unless we receive payment in full within [Number] days of the date of this letter, our client reserves the right to commence legal proceedings without further notice."

## Practical Use
Fulfilling pre-action requirements and attempting settlement before litigation.
