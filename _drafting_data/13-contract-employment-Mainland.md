## System
## System: Mainland

## Authority Tier
Primary Statute (Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021)

## Language Variants
Arabic (Primary), English (Dual-language MOHRE standard)

## Currency Warning
Must use the 2021 Labor Law regime. Old "Unlimited" contracts are obsolete.

## Template Type
Employment Agreement

## Structural Skeleton
1. Fixed Term Duration
2. Job Description
3. Basic Salary and Allowances
4. Probation Period (Max 6 months)
5. Annual Leave and Sick Leave
6. End of Service Gratuity (or Pension for UAE/GCC nationals)
7. Non-Compete Clause (Restricted under Art. 10)
8. Termination with Notice

## Drafting Conventions
- Use MOHRE Standard Template as the baseline.
- All contracts must be registered with MOHRE.
- Wages must be paid via WPS (Wage Protection System).

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The employment contract shall be concluded for a fixed term not exceeding three years, and may be renewed for a similar or shorter term one or more times."

## Practical Use
Standard employment for mainland Dubai private sector companies.
