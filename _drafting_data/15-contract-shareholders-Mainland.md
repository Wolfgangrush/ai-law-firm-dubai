## System
## System: Mainland

## Authority Tier
Primary Statute (Federal Decree-Law No. 32 of 2021)

## Language Variants
Arabic (Primary), English (Translation)

## Currency Warning
2021 Companies Law removed the 51% local ownership requirement for many activities.

## Template Type
Shareholders' Agreement / Memorandum of Association (MOA)

## Structural Skeleton
1. Capital Structure
2. Management Rights (Manager vs Board)
3. Profit and Loss Distribution
4. Pre-emption Rights
5. Deadlock Provisions
6. Termination and Dissolution
7. Governing Law (UAE Federal)
8. Jurisdiction (Dubai Courts)

## Drafting Conventions
- SHA often sits alongside the notarized MOA.
- Ensure no conflict with mandatory provisions of the 2021 Companies Law.
- Side agreements must be carefully drafted to avoid "Nominee" law issues (though largely relaxed).

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The partners may appoint one or more managers for the company from among the partners or others, and the MOA shall determine the powers of the manager."

## Practical Use
Structuring LLCs in mainland Dubai.
