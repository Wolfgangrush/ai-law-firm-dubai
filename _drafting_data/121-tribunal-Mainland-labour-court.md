## Currency Warning
Mainland UAE Labour Law modernised by Federal Decree-Law 33/2021 (replacing Federal Law 8/1980). MoHRE procedures govern complaints. Mandatory MoHRE conciliation before Labour Court. Labour Courts at Dubai Courts have specialist Labour Departments.

## Template Type
Tribunal Complaint + Court Filing

## Structural Skeleton

**(A) MOHRE COMPLAINT** (شكوى وزارة الموارد البشرية والتوطين)

1. **Online filing** via MoHRE portal or in-person at MoHRE service centre.
2. **Employee identification** — name · Emirates ID · employment details (start date · job title · salary · contract terms).
3. **Employer identification** — establishment card / trade license · address · authorised representative.
4. **Complaint particulars**:
   - Type of dispute — unpaid salary · gratuity · termination · overtime · leave entitlements · arbitrary dismissal.
   - Sum claimed (AED).
   - Factual narrative.
5. **Documents** — employment contract (DIFC vs onshore distinction) · payslips · termination letter · correspondence.
6. **Mandatory MoHRE conciliation** — typically within 2-3 weeks.
7. **CRC (Claim Referral Certificate)** issued if not settled → gateway to Labour Court.

**(B) LABOUR COURT FILING**

1. **Heading** — Court of First Instance · Labour Division · Dubai Courts · case number · year.
2. **Title** — "Labour Claim".
3. **Parties** — employee + employer.
4. **MoHRE conciliation evidence** — CRC attached.
5. **Claims**:
   - Unpaid salary · gratuity · overtime · leave pay · arbitrary dismissal compensation.
   - Federal Decree-Law 33/2021 specific provisions invoked.
6. **Quantum + computation** — detailed.
7. **Reliefs sought**:
   - Payment of claimed sums.
   - Compensation for arbitrary dismissal (up to 3 months' salary).
   - End-of-service gratuity (Art 51).
   - Interest + costs.
8. **Annexures**.

## Drafting Conventions
- **MoHRE conciliation mandatory** — Labour Court will not entertain claim without CRC.
- **1-year limitation** — strict; from termination or accrual of cause of action.
- **End-of-service gratuity** — 21 days' basic salary per year for first 5 years · 30 days' basic salary per year thereafter.
- **Arbitrary dismissal** (الفصل التعسفي) — Art 47 Federal Decree-Law 33/2021: compensation up to 3 months' salary.
- **Federal Decree-Law 9/2024 amendments** — new provisions on remote work · flexible work arrangements · maternity protections.
- **Foreign workers + work permits** — Ministry permits + cancellation procedures.
- **Domestic workers** — separate regime under Federal Law 10/2017.
- **DIFC employment** — separate DIFC Employment Law 2019; DIFC SCT (file 120) jurisdiction for DIFC employment disputes.
- **No costs of litigation** awarded typically.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"An employee whose employment is terminated arbitrarily shall be entitled to compensation not exceeding three months' total wage. Disputes shall first be referred to the competent authority (MoHRE) for mediation. If not settled, the dispute may be referred to the competent court." (Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021, Articles 47 + 54, paraphrased.)

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Mainland Dubai labour disputes are high-volume for solo practice. The MoHRE conciliation stage settles most matters; Labour Court is the formal escalation. Solo practitioners must distinguish DIFC employment (DIFC SCT or DIFC Court of First Instance under DIFC Employment Law 2019) vs Mainland (MoHRE + Dubai Labour Court under Federal Decree-Law 33/2021). The 1-year limitation is strict.
