## Template Type
Court Application - Joinder of Parties (Idkhal Khasm)

## Structural Skeleton
1. Header (Case No.)
2. Identification of the New Party
3. Legal/Factual Basis for Joinder
4. Request for Notification of the New Party

## Drafting Conventions
- Can be requested by a party or ordered by the court ("Ex officio").
- Must be related to the same subject matter.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"The Claimant requests the honorable Court to join [Party Name] as a party to the case to enable the Court to issue a comprehensive judgment that binds all parties involved."

## Practical Use
Expanding the scope of a mainland Dubai lawsuit to include necessary parties.
