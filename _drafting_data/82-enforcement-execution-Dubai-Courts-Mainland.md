## System
## System: Mainland

## Authority Tier
Primary Statute (Federal Decree-Law No. 42 of 2022, Arts 230-330 Execution Provisions)

## Language Variants
Arabic (Mandatory)

## Currency Warning
Mainland UAE execution under Federal Decree-Law 42/2022 (replacing earlier Federal Law 11/1992). Dubai Courts have specialist Execution Court (محكمة التنفيذ) for enforcement of judgments. Execution procedures distinct from DIFC; do NOT use DIFC procedure terminology in Mainland filings.

## Template Type
Application / Procedural Document

## Structural Skeleton

**Application to Open Execution File** (طلب فتح ملف تنفيذ)

1. **Heading** — Dubai Courts · Execution Court · case number · parties.
2. **Title** — "Application to Open Execution File on Judgment of [Court] dated [Date]".
3. **Judgment particulars**:
   - Court of origin.
   - Case number + date.
   - Judgment terms (sum · interest · costs).
   - Finality confirmation.
4. **Service of judgment** — evidence judgment was duly served on debtor.
5. **Sum due**:
   - Principal (in AED).
   - Interest (Federal Decree-Law 50/2022 + Civil Code Art 88).
   - Court fees + execution costs.
   - Partial payments offset (if any).
6. **Debtor's identifiers**:
   - Name · Emirates ID / commercial license.
   - Address (residential + business).
   - Bank accounts known (if any).
7. **Reliefs sought**:
   - Open execution file.
   - Issue execution warrant.
   - Order specific execution measures.

**Execution Measures Available**

1. **Attachment of bank accounts** (حجز الحسابات البنكية).
2. **Attachment of salary** (حجز الراتب) — subject to UAE Labour Law minimum protections.
3. **Attachment of real estate** (حجز العقارات) — registered with Dubai Land Department (DLD).
4. **Attachment of vehicles** (حجز السيارات) — registered with RTA.
5. **Travel ban** (منع السفر) — order to immigration authorities restricting debtor's departure from UAE; common but increasingly restricted.
6. **Asset attachment** (حجز الأموال) — movable property at debtor's premises.
7. **Imprisonment for debt** (الحبس لعدم السداد) — limited; primarily for cheque cases under UAE Penal Code; substantially curtailed post-2022 reforms.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Arabic mandatory** — all execution filings in Arabic.
- **Sealed judgment + execution copy** — only sealed-by-court copy enforceable.
- **Travel ban** — increasingly restricted post-2022 reforms; requires substantive grounds.
- **Federal Decree-Law 50/2022 (Commercial Transactions)** — interest rules + execution.
- **Cheque cases** — separate criminal track under UAE Penal Code; decriminalised post-2022 except for fraud.
- **DIFC Court judgments** — enforced via DIFC-Dubai Enforcement Protocol (file 80); no re-litigation.
- **Foreign judgments** — recognised under Federal Decree-Law 42/2022 Chapter 5 (Recognition + Enforcement) subject to:
  - Foreign court had jurisdiction per UAE rules.
  - Judgment final + conclusive.
  - No conflicting UAE judgment.
  - No violation of UAE public policy.
  - Reciprocity.
- **NYC awards** — arbitral awards seated abroad enforceable under New York Convention (UAE acceded 2006).
- **Asset trace** — investigation via bank inquiries + DLD + RTA + ADGM Commercial Registry.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
"Execution of judgments shall be carried out by the Execution Court. The judgment creditor shall file an application for execution accompanied by the sealed copy of the judgment. The Execution Court may order such measures as are necessary including attachment of assets, garnishment of debts, and other coercive measures." (Federal Decree-Law No. 42 of 2022, Arts 230-235, paraphrased.)

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Mainland Dubai Courts execution is the principal enforcement route for onshore Dubai debts. Solo practitioners must (a) translate all documents to Arabic; (b) identify debtor's bank accounts + assets pre-execution; (c) file at Execution Court; (d) navigate the post-2022 reformed regime (travel ban + imprisonment restricted). For DIFC Court judgments enforcing against onshore debtors, use DIFC-Dubai Protocol (file 80).
