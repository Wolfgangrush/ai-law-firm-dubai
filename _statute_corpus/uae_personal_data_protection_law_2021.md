# UAE PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION LAW
# FEDERAL DECREE-LAW NO. 45 OF 2021

## PART 1: SCOPE

### Articles 2 & 3: Application
Applies to data controllers/processors in the UAE and has **extraterritorial reach** for data of UAE residents.
- **Exemptions:** Government data, personal/household use, health/credit data (sectoral), and specific Free Zones (DIFC/ADGM).

---

## PART 2: DATA PROTECTION PRINCIPLES

### Article 4: Consent
Processing is generally prohibited without the data subject's **consent**, unless an exception applies.

---

## PART 3: DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

### Articles 13-18: Rights
- Right to Access.
- Right to Data Portability.
- Right to Rectification and Erasure (Forgotten).
- Right to Object (especially for direct marketing).

---

## PART 4: OBLIGATIONS

### Article 16: Data Protection Officer (DPO)
Mandatory for high-risk technologies or large-scale sensitive data monitoring.

### Article 20: Impact Assessment (DPIA)
Required for high-risk processing or new technologies.

---

## PART 5: DATA BREACHES

### Article 9: Notification
Controllers must notify the **UAE Data Office** immediately of breaches posing a risk to privacy or security.

---

## PART 6: CROSS-BORDER TRANSFERS

### Articles 22 & 23: Transfers
Permitted to countries with "adequate protection" or via Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

---

## REGULATOR
**UAE Data Office** (established under Law No. 44 of 2021).
