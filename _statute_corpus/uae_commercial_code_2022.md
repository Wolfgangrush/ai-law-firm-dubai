# UAE COMMERCIAL CODE
# FEDERAL DECREE-LAW NO. 50 OF 2022

## PART 1: GENERAL

### Article 5: Commercial Activities
Includes **Virtual Assets** as recognized commercial activities.

### Article 6: Digital Economy
Includes creation and management of electronic platforms, websites, and AI-related activities as commercial acts.

### Article 11: Definition of Merchant
Any person habitually engaging in commercial activities, including **digital and virtual operators**.

---

## PART 2: COMMERCIAL OBLIGATIONS

### Article 72: Interest Cap
Default interest rate (if not specified) is capped at **9% per annum**.

### Article 88: Prohibition of Compounding
 charging compound interest (interest on interest) on commercial loans is explicitly prohibited.

### Article 94: Statute of Limitations
Claims between merchants arising from commercial obligations must be brought within **5 years** (reduced from 10 years).

---

## PART 5: COMMERCIAL GUARANTEES

### Article 475: Bank Guarantees
Banks may refrain from paying a beneficiary if proceeds are subject to a court-ordered attachment.

---

## PART 6: ISLAMIC FINANCE
Provides a dedicated legal framework for Sharia-compliant transactions with Islamic Financial Institutions.

---

## AGE OF MAJORITY

### Article 18: Trading Age
Age of majority for trade is **21 years**. Minors (18+) require court permission.
