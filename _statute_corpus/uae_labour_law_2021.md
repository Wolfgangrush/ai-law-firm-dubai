# UAE LABOUR LAW
# FEDERAL DECREE-LAW NO. 33 OF 2021

## PART 1: GENERAL

### Article 1: Title
Cited as the "Labour Law 2021".

### Article 8: Fixed-Term Contracts
All employment contracts must be **fixed-term**. Maximum duration typically 3 years (renewable).

---

## PART 2: WORKING MODELS

### Article 7: Working Models
Recognizes five models:
1. Full-time
2. Part-time
3. Temporary Work
4. Flexible Work
5. Remote Work

---

## PART 3: WORKING HOURS AND OVERTIME

### Article 17: Maximum Hours
8 hours per day or 48 hours per week.

### Article 19: Overtime
Limited to 2 hours per day. Compensation is basic wage + 25% (min) or 50% (if night/rest day).

---

## PART 4: LEAVES AND ENTITLEMENTS

### Article 29: Annual Leave
30 days per year for service > 1 year.

### Article 30: Maternity Leave
60 days (45 days full pay, 15 days half pay).

### Article 32: Compassionate & Paternal Leave
- Compassionate: 3-5 days.
- Paternal: 5 working days.

---

## PART 5: PROBATION AND NOTICE

### Article 9: Probation
Maximum 6 months. 14 days' notice required from Employer to terminate.

### Article 43: Notice Period
After probation, 30 to 90 days.

---

## PART 6: END OF SERVICE GRATUITY

### Article 51: Calculation
Full-time foreign workers:
- 21 days' basic wage for first 5 years.
- 30 days' basic wage for years thereafter.
- Must be paid within 14 days of termination.

---

## PART 9: TERMINATION

### Article 44: Summary Dismissal
Employer can fire without notice for specific cases (misconduct, false identity, gross financial loss).

### Article 45: Resignation without Notice
Employee can leave without notice if employer breaches contract or assaults employee.

---

## PART 10: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### Article 55: Fee Exemption
Claims <= AED 100,000 are exempt from judicial fees at all stages.
