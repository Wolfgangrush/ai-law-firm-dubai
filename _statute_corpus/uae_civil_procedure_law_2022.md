# UAE CIVIL PROCEDURE LAW
# FEDERAL DECREE-LAW NO. 42 OF 2022

## PART 1: GENERAL

### Article 5: Language
Arabic is the official language. However, the Court may decide that **English** be used for specific specialized cases.

---

## PART 2: JURISDICTION

### Article 29: Single-Judge Competence
Single-judge courts hear cases regardless of value.

### Finality Threshold
Judgments are final (not appealable) if the claim value is <= **AED 50,000**.

---

## PART 3: LITIGATION PROCESS

### Article 78: Timelines
Courts must issue judgments within **80 days** from the first hearing.

### Article 11: Service of Process
Permits service via modern technology (audio/video calls, SMS, email, smart apps).

---

## PART 4: APPEALS

### Article 167: Deliberation Chambers
Court of Appeal can decide directly in chambers (without public hearing) within 20 working days for specific cases.

---

## PART 5: EXECUTION

### Bounced Cheques
Beneficiaries can seek direct enforcement of a bounced cheque's value through the execution judge without a full lawsuit.

### Article 177: Stay of Execution
Court of Cassation must decide on stay requests within 15 business days.
