# UAE PENAL CODE
# FEDERAL DECREE-LAW NO. 31 OF 2021

## PART 1: GENERAL

### Article 27-30: Classification of Crimes
1. **Felonies:** Punishable by death, life imprisonment, or temporary imprisonment.
2. **Misdemeanors:** Punishable by confinement (jail) or fine > AED 10,000.
3. **Infractions:** Minor violations.

### Article 65: Corporate Liability
Legal persons (companies) are criminally liable for crimes committed by representatives. Fines up to **AED 5,000,000**.

---

## PART 2: SPECIFIC OFFENCES

### Article 276: Private Sector Bribery
Accepting bribes in the private sector can lead to up to 5 years in prison.

### Article 451: Fraud
Punishable by jail or fine for unlawful appropriation of property through deception.

### Article 453: Breach of Trust
Penalizes embezzlement or misuse of property delivered under trust/contract.

### Bounced Cheques
Insufficient funds cases are largely **decriminalized** unless bad faith or fraudulent intent is proven.

---

## SOCIAL REFORMS
- **Consensual Relationships:** Decriminalized for adults (18+).
- **Alcohol:** Penalties removed for consumption in authorized/private areas for adults (21+).
- **Suicide Attempt:** Decriminalized; emphasis on psychological treatment.

---

## JUVENILE LIABILITY

### Article 1: Age of Liability
No criminal action against children under **12 years of age**.
- Minors (12-18) subject to reformatory measures.
