# DIFC REAL PROPERTY LAW
# DIFC LAW NO. 1 OF 2018

## PART 1: GENERAL

### Article 1: Title
Cited as the "Real Property Law 2018".

### Article 3: Application
Applies to real property within the jurisdiction of the DIFC.

---

## PART 2: REGISTRATION

### Article 6: Land Registry
Establishes the DIFC Land Registry.

### Article 7: Mandatory Registration
All interests in real property (freehold, leasehold > 1 year) must be registered to be legally effective.

---

## PART 3: OWNERSHIP AND TENURE

### Article 10: Types of Ownership
- **Freehold:** Absolute ownership.
- **Leasehold:** Ownership for a fixed term.

---

## PART 5: COMMON PROPERTY (STRATA)

### Article 35: Strata Management
Provisions for the management of common areas in multi-unit developments and the establishment of "Owners' Associations".

---

## PART 7: PROTECTION OF PURCHASERS

### Article 50: Off-Plan Protections
Specific requirements for developers selling off-plan units, including escrow account mandates.

---

## PRACTICAL IMPLICATION
Transactions follow a common-law conveyancing process. Practitioners must verify title via the DIFC Land Registry.
