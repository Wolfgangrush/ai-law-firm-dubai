# UAE ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AML) LAW
# FEDERAL DECREE-LAW NO. 20 OF 2018 / NO. 10 OF 2025

## PART 1: DEFINITIONS

### Article 2: Money Laundering
Transferring, concealing, or using proceeds derived from a "Predicate Offence" with knowledge of their illegal origin.

### Predicate Offences
Includes any act constituting a felony or misdemeanor under UAE law (all-crimes approach). **Tax Evasion** and **Proliferation Financing** are explicitly included.

---

## PART 2: LIABILITY

### Article 3: Corporate Liability
Companies are criminally responsible for AML/CFT crimes committed in their name.

---

## PART 3: OVERSIGHT

### Article 9: Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU)
Central Bank unit responsible for analyzing **Suspicious Transaction Reports (STRs)**.

### Article 5: Freezing Powers
The Central Bank Governor can freeze suspicious funds for up to **7 working days** without a court order.

---

## PART 4: COMPLIANCE OBLIGATIONS

### Articles 14-16: Requirements
- **Customer Due Diligence (CDD):** ID verification and beneficial owner tracking.
- **Reporting:** Mandatory STR filing via **goAML** portal.
- **Record Keeping:** Must keep data for at least **5 years**.
- **Compliance Officer:** Mandatory appointment for DNFBPs (Designated Non-Financial Businesses and Professions).

---

## PENALTIES
- **Fines (Individuals):** AED 100,000 to AED 5,000,000.
- **Fines (Legal Entities):** Up to **AED 100,000,000**.
- **Confiscation:** Mandatory for proceeds or equivalent value.
