# UAE EVIDENCE LAW
# FEDERAL DECREE-LAW NO. 35 OF 2022

## PART 1: GENERAL

### Article 2: Burden of Proof
Lies with the claimant. Respondent's denial must be made under oath.

### Article 4: Form of Proof
Obligations do not require a specific form unless stipulated by law or prior agreement.

---

## PART 2: ELECTRONIC EVIDENCE

### Article 53: Definition
Includes data generated/stored via IT (emails, WhatsApp, digital recordings, social media).

### Article 54: Probative Value
Digital evidence has the same legal weight as traditional physical documents.

---

## PART 3: WITNESS TESTIMONY

### Articles 65-85
Witnesses can establish facts where written evidence is unavailable or lost. Court has broad discretion.

### Remote Testimony
Permitted via video conferencing (Article 21).

---

## PART 5: EXPERTISE

### Articles 109-125
Court appoints technical/financial experts. Parties can agree on a specific expert for court approval.

---

## PART 6: OATHS

### Articles 93-106
- **Decisive Oath:** Directed by one party to another to end the dispute.
- **Supplemental Oath:** Directed by the judge.

---

## FINES

### Article 43: Unfounded Forgery Claims
Court can impose fines between **AED 3,000 and AED 10,000** for false forgery challenges.
