# UAE COMMERCIAL COMPANIES LAW
# FEDERAL DECREE-LAW NO. 32 OF 2021 / NO. 20 OF 2025

## PART 1: GENERAL

### Article 8: Definition
A company is a contract where persons participate in a profit-making project. Allows for **Single-Person Companies**.

### Article 9: Recognized Forms
1. General Partnership
2. Limited Partnership
3. Limited Liability Company (LLC)
4. Public Joint Stock Company (PJSC)
5. Private Joint Stock Company (PrJSC)

---

## PART 2: NEW VEHICLES

### SPACs & SPVs
Introduced legal frameworks for Special Purpose Acquisition Companies and Special Purpose Vehicles.

---

## PART 3: LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES (LLC)

### Article 71: Management
Managers whose term expires continue for up to 6 months until a replacement is appointed.

---

## PART 4: JOINT STOCK COMPANIES

### Article 118: Board Nationality
Removed the requirement for majority UAE national board members (unless for "strategic impact" activities).

### Share Classes (2025 Amendment)
Allows companies to issue multiple classes of shares (e.g., weighted voting rights).

---

## PART 5: EXIT & RE-DOMICILIATION

### Redomiciliation (2025 Amendment)
Explicitly allows migration between free zones and mainland or from outside the UAE.

### Shareholder Rights
Formalizes **Drag-along** and **Tag-along** rights in the statute.

---

## COMPLIANCE

### Liquidation
Creditor claim period reduced to **30 days**.
